About Me


Hello, I'm Asia - a designer. Who’s looking to further my career in design. I was born in Illinois but I was raised in Missouri, Kansas, and Texas. Relocating in my earlier years and witnessing different cultures has helped me to adapt to new environments.

I've always been exceedingly curious. With my unconventional and provocative viewpoint, I’m always thinking ahead and creating fresh ideas. I’ve grown to process my experiences in a logical manner, but I have the ability to be highly rational and incredibly empathetic.

I began my journey at The University of Phoenix for business administration.That is where my interest in psychology and human design developed. Afterwards, I attended The Art Institute of St. Louis, Mo., where I enrolled into Photography courses, where my passion for color theory, design, and aesthetics blossomed.

I realized I’m most fulfilled when I can express my creativity. I am doing what I love by completing courses with Generation USA and Udemy to assist me to give my best contribution and become a designer to create innovative designs and user experiences.
